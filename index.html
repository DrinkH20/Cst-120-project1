About Me

Hello! My name is Joel Jones, and this is my website. I was born and raised in the beautiful city of Vancouver, Washington, located just across the river from Portland, Oregon. Vancouver has always been my home - I grew up here with my large family of nine siblings. I am the youngest of the family, so I always had a lot of older brothers and sisters to look up to and learn from as I was growing up. My parents have lived in Washington for their whole lives. I also have many aunts and uncles who live in this area near my family.

Now that I am finishing up high school and am looking ahead to my future career options, I find myself feeling uncertain about which path is right for me. For the past couple of years, I have been very interested in getting into software development or engineering. I am good with computers and logical thinking, and I like the idea of creatively solving problems and building useful products and apps that people use every day. However, as I have researched the tech industry more lately, I have felt somewhat discouraged. With outsourcing, automation, and shifting H-1B visa policies, the job security and opportunities in that field do not seem as stable as they once did compared to other options I am considering. I do not want to invest years of training in a career that could leave me struggling to find a job. So, for now, I remain open-minded about different career paths as I work and volunteer in various areas that interest me. I have been researching many of the trades, specifically plumbing, but I do not know exactly what God is calling me to do. My goal is to find something I am passionate about that will also provide me with a stable, fulfilling lifestyle long-term.
